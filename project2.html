Diabetes Prediction Website

This Diabetes Prediction Website uses advanced machine learning to predict an individual's risk of developing diabetes based on health and demographic data. The user-friendly platform provides personalized, accurate risk assessments, promoting early awareness and proactive health management. Its goal is to reduce the incidence of diabetes through accessible technology.

PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Diabetes Prediction Website uses advanced machine learning techniques, such as regression and ensemble models, to help users assess their diabetes risk and gain insights into their health. By providing personalized risk assessments, it empowers users to take proactive steps toward preventive healthcare. This platform represents a key innovation in health technology.
